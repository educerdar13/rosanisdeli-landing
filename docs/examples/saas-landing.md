## Design System Generated

- **Colors:** Deep Navy (#1B2A4A), Off-white (#FAFAF8), Coral accent (#E8654A), Cool Gray (#6B7280)
- **Headline font:** Space Grotesk (geometric, modern)
- **Body font:** DM Sans
- **Layout:** Clean hero with headline and product preview mockup. Features in a 2-column alternating layout (text left/visual right, then switch). Social proof strip with logos.
